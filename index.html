Tribute to Gautama Buddha

Short Biography

  • Gautama Buddha was birth in Lumbini, capital city of the ancient Sakia Kingdom, near actual Nepal.
  • Was the successor of the throne.
  • He was married when he have sixteen years old with your own cousin named Yasodhara.
  • At the age of 29 he resigned his family and royal inheritance
  • He travelled for distant places to his city of birth to find the way of suppressing the pain.
  • After experiencing many forms of different spirituality and without achieving successfully his principal goal he decides to do his own way up to managing to suppress the pain.
  • Having managed to suppress the pain one dedicates to sharing his learnings up to achieving the condition of complete lighting, called "Nirvana", moment in which your spirit completes his earthly evolution and disappears of the world.
